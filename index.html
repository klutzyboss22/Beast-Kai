Wiki

Battle Simulator

Rules

Battle Format: Double Battles

Level: 50

HP Formula

HP = 100 + (Health Stat × 3)

Damage Formula

Damage = ((((24 + Level) × Move Power × Atk Stat) / (32 × Def Stat)) + 8) × Targets × Weather × STAB × Type × Other × Random

Accuracy

  • AC ≥ 110 → always hits
  • Otherwise: Move AC × (100 / Target Evasion)
  • Min 35% / Max 100%

Core Mechanics

  • Blocking reduces damage (not negates)
  • Switching happens at end of turn
  • Terrains override each other
  • Dual-type moves multiply effectiveness (max 4×)
